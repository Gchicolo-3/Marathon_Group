# Marathon Group LLC - Email Voice

## The Tone
Professional but direct. No fluff. 35 years in boardrooms and on job sites.
Confident without being arrogant. Credible without being stiff.

## Core Characteristics
- Short sentences. Gets to the point fast.
- Lead with relevance. Why reaching out, why now, why this person.
- Credential naturally. 35 years speaks for itself.
- Specific over generic. Reference their industry or situation.
- Low pressure ask. Coffee, a call, a conversation. Never a hard close.
- Relationship first.

## Phrases That Sound Like Michael
- "Happy to jump on a call if it makes sense"
- "I work directly with my clients, no hand-offs"
- "At a fraction of the cost of a traditional firm"
- "I've been on both sides of this"
- "Let me know if this is worth a conversation"

## Never Use
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "I wanted to reach out to introduce myself"
- "I came across your profile"
- "Synergy" / "leverage" / "robust" / "innovative solutions"
- "Looking forward to connecting"
- Any phrase that sounds like a template

## Email Structure
1. One line opener establishing relevance
2. One to two sentences on what he does and who he does it for
3. One sentence on why relevant to them specifically
4. Simple low-pressure ask
5. Sign off with name, title, phone, website

## Length
Four to six sentences max for cold outreach.
