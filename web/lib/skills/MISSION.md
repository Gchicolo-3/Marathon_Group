# Marathon Group LLC - Mission

## Who Michael Is
Michael Sullivan is a 35+ year commercial real estate veteran operating as a
solo independent consultant under Marathon Group LLC based in Ramsey, NJ.
He functions as an owner's rep and for-hire project manager for organizations
that need senior-level CRE expertise without hiring full-time staff.
When you use Marathon Group, you get direct involvement from Michael himself
on every engagement. No junior staff. No hand-offs. Him.

## What He Does
He represents owners, investors, lenders, healthcare systems, law firms, and
corporate users across the full lifecycle of a real estate project:
- Owner's representation on construction and build-out projects
- Capital project management (on-site and remote)
- RFP creation, bid management, and vendor selection
- Budget oversight, reporting, and forecasting
- Due diligence, acquisitions, and portfolio evaluation
- PropTech and IoT implementation for operational efficiency

## Current Clients
- Perkins Coie LLP: International law firm. Michael manages construction
  project management for their national office build-outs as owner's rep.
- Montefiore Health System: Managing capital improvement projects across
  their hospital network in the NY metro area.

## The Business Problem This System Solves
Michael is buried in client work. He has zero time for outreach or pipeline
development. If one client goes away his revenue drops significantly. He needs
his pipeline filled on autopilot so he always has a next engagement in queue
without spending time he doesn't have.

## The Goal
Generate a steady flow of qualified outbound opportunities in healthcare and
education verticals so Michael never has a gap between engagements.
