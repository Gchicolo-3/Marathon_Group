# Marathon Group LLC - 26-Week Campaign

## Campaign Logic
- Week 1: Cold introduction. Who Michael is, what he does, why relevant.
- Weeks 2-4: Value delivery. Pain points, case studies, insights.
- Weeks 5-8: Social proof. Client results, project examples, credibility.
- Weeks 9-13: Nurture. Market insights, PropTech trends, relevant news.
- Weeks 14-18: Re-engagement. Different angle, new hook, fresh ask.
- Weeks 19-22: Direct value offer. Free consultation, portfolio review.
- Weeks 23-26: Final sequence. Last touch, referral ask, stay in network.

## Healthcare Track Topics
- Capital project management
- Facilities modernization
- Cost reduction through vendor management
- Compliance and regulatory project oversight
- PropTech and IoT for operational efficiency

## Education Track Topics
- Campus renovation and expansion
- Deferred maintenance project management
- Owner's rep for construction projects
- Budget oversight and reporting
- Vendor selection and bid management

## Rules
- Never send more than one email per week per prospect
- Always personalize opener based on prospect situation
- If prospect replies, remove from sequence and flag for Michael immediately
- Week 1 emails always go to Gmail drafts for Michael approval
- Weeks 2-26 can send automatically if Michael enables auto-send
